Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • Paneer – 200g
  • Tomato Puree – 2 large
  • Onion – 1 (chopped)
  • Cream – 3 tbsp
  • Butter – 2 tbsp
  • Cashews – 10
  • Spices – Chili powder, Garam Masala, Salt.

Process:

Cook onion-tomato base, blend with cashew paste, add cream, spices, and paneer. Simmer and serve with naan or rice.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas – 1 cup (boiled)
  • Onion – 1
  • Tomato – 2
  • Ginger-Garlic – 1 tbsp
  • Spices – Chole Masala, Cumin, Chili powder

Process:

Sauté onions and tomatoes, add spices and boiled chickpeas. Cook for 10-15 minutes until thick.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken – 300g (boneless)
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Tomato Puree – 2
  • Cream – 4 tbsp
  • Butter – 3 tbsp
  • Spices – Garam masala, Chili powder

Process:

Marinate chicken, grill or fry, then cook in buttery tomato-cream gravy.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice – 1 cup
  • Mixed Veggies – 1 cup
  • Yogurt – ½ cup
  • Spices – Biryani Masala, Bay Leaf, Cloves
  • Saffron – optional

Process:

Cook vegetables with spices, layer with partially cooked rice, seal and steam (dum) for 15 mins.
